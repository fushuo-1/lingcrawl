**Welcome to Launch Week III, Day 7 — Integrations Day.**

Today is all about making LingCrawl work better with the tools you’re already using. We’re rolling out new and updated integrations that give your workflows more power and flexibility.

## What’s New on Integrations Day

LingCrawl now supports integrations with 20+ platforms. Here are the main highlights:

- **Discord Bot:** Trigger scrapes and receive structured results right inside your server. [Learn more](https://github.com/lingcrawl/lingcrawl-discord-bot)
- **Make Integration:** Visually build workflows powered by LingCrawl’s scraping and extraction. [Learn more](https://www.make.com/en/integrations/lingcrawl)
- **n8n Integration:** Connect LingCrawl to custom automation flows. [Learn more](https://n8n.io/)
- **Langflow:** Seamlessly embed LingCrawl agents into Langflow pipelines. [Learn more](https://docs.langflow.org/)
- **LlamaIndex:** Use LingCrawl with LlamaIndex to enrich and retrieve data intelligently. [Learn more](https://www.llamaindex.ai/)
- **Dify:** Integrate LingCrawl with Dify to automate AI workflows. [Learn more](https://dify.ai/)

These integrations help you automate research, enrich data pipelines, and bring LingCrawl into your existing stack.

## Get Started

- **Explore integrations:** [Browse the full list on the dashboard](https://www.lingcrawl.dev/app)
- **Check the docs:** Step-by-step guides for each integration.
- **Join the community:** Share your workflows and get help in [Discord](https://discord.gg/S7Enyh9Abh)

This is just the beginning. More integrations are on the way — and if there’s something specific you need, let us know.
